About Us

Founded in Westmoreland, a parish known for its breathtaking coastlines, lush farmlands, and grounded people, RUne State blends island craftsmanship with bold innovation. Here, where cane fields meet cliffs and rivers cut paths through red soil, we saw an opportunity to carve a new identity into timekeeping — one that reflects the resilience, soul, and authenticity of Jamaican life.

Each RUne State watch begins with intent:

Our Story Westmoreland, with its stunning coastlines, rolling cane fields, and close-knit communities, inspired us to craft something unique — a timepiece that honors our culture and tells a story with every tick. The name RUne State draws from the ancient tradition of runes — symbols carved into stone that captured history and wisdom. Like those runes, our watches are designed to carry meaning, legacy, and identity forward through time.

  • Locally inspired materials, textures, and color palettes that echo the land — from the golds of a Negril sunset to the deep blacks of rich soil.
  • Minimalist, rugged design representing both strength and elegance — like the Jamaican people who carry themselves with both pride and grace.
  • Unapologetically bold names and models inspired by old Jamaican sayings, Westmoreland districts, and forgotten Maroon legends.

Community

Our Commitment to Community We’re not just about watches — we’re about people. A percentage of every sale supports youth programs in Westmoreland, empowering the next generation to dream big and achieve greatness. When you wear RUne State, you’re joining a movement that values heritage, purpose, and progress.
