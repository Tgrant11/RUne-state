Our Services

At RUne State, we don’t just make watches — we craft legacy. Rooted in the soul of Westmoreland, Jamaica, our brand is built on precision, pride, and purpose. Every RUne State piece is more than a timekeeper; it's a bold expression of identity, a tribute to culture, and a commitment to quality. Whether you're walking the streets of Savanna-la-Mar or presenting in a global boardroom, RUne State is your reminder to move with intention, lead with authenticity, and wear your story with pride.

  • Custom Watch Engraving: Personalize your timepiece with names, dates, or phrases for a lasting legacy.
  • Bespoke Watch Design (Limited Edition): Collaborate with us to create one-of-a-kind watches inspired by Jamaican roots and culture.
  • Watch Care & Maintenance: Battery replacement, strap adjustments, and tropical-weather protection to keep your watch in top form.
  • Heritage Packaging Service: Culturally inspired packaging including maps, proverbs, and origin cards for a meaningful unboxing experience.
  • Cultural Collaborations & Drops: Special editions made in partnership with Jamaican artists and creatives, supporting local talent.
  • Youth Empowerment Workshops: Design and entrepreneurship sessions for Westmoreland’s youth — building the next generation of visionaries.
  • Watch Restoration for Legacy Pieces: Restore older RUne State watches and pass them on through generations with pride.
  • Lifestyle Branding & Styling Consultations: Get personalized advice on how to style your RUne State for every moment — from beach to boardroom.
